Privacy Policy

FamilyFlow respects your privacy:

  • All data is stored locally on your device

  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No cloud storage or data transmission

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new family member?

Go to Profiles tab → Add Child button → Enter name and details

How do I create recurring activities?

Go to Calendar → + button → Select "Recurring" → Choose days

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings → Allow Notifications toggle
