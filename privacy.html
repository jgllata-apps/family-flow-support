Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2024

1. Information We Collect

FamilyFlow is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Names: User and family member names for profile creation
  • Photos (Optional): Profile pictures if you choose to add them
  • Activities & Tasks: Calendar events, tasks, and reminders you create
  • App Settings: Language preferences and notification settings

2. How We Use Your Information

All information is used solely for app functionality:

  • Display family profiles and calendars
  • Show scheduled activities and reminders
  • Provide notifications when requested
  • Remember your language and settings preferences

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We do not:

  • ❌ Store data on external servers
  • ❌ Use cloud synchronization
  • ❌ Transmit data over the internet
  • ❌ Have access to your information

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features including device passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Period.

  • No third-party services
  • No analytics companies
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers

5. Children's Privacy

FamilyFlow is designed for family use and may contain information about children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Children's data stays on the family device
  • No data collection from children
  • Parents control all information entry
  • Compliant with COPPA requirements

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Edit: Modify or update any information
  • Delete: Remove individual items or reset all data
  • Export: Data remains on your device

7. Notifications

FamilyFlow can send local notifications for reminders and activities:

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device
  • You control notification permissions in Settings
  • No data is sent to notification services

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through app updates. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. International Users

FamilyFlow supports multiple languages and can be used worldwide. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, no international data transfers occur.
